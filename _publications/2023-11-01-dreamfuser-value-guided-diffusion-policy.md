- [OpenReview](https://openreview.net/forum?id=9jmUwjZi7j)

DreamFuser is a trajectory-based value optimization approach that integrates diffusion-based trajectory learning with efficient Q-function learning. It addresses computational challenges in action sampling during training by leveraging the Generalized Noisy Action Markov Decision Process (GNMDP), which treats the diffusion denoising process as part of the MDP transition.
