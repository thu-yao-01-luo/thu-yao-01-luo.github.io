- [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.18903)

This work studies curriculum-based LLM pretraining under mixed-quality data and identifies a key incompatibility between ascending-quality curricula and decaying learning rates. It shows that moderate learning-rate decay together with model averaging can recover the gains of curriculum ordering.
